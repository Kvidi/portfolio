About Me

I'm a C#/.NET developer with a strong interest in building both backend and frontend solutions.

After completing a full-stack developer program at Lexicon, I've gained hands-on experience with technologies such as C#, .NET, ASP.NET Core, SQL/Entity Framework, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and Bootstrap.
During the program I worked in agile teams following Scrum, including a final internship-project where we developed an online auction web application — an experience that gave me valuable insight into the full development cycle, from planning and design to implementation.

Before transitioning into IT, I built a long career in the food industry where I also held union representative roles. Through that experience, I developed strengths in responsibility, problem-solving and teamwork — qualities I now bring into my work as a developer.

I enjoy structure, clear goals and continuous learning, and I'm motivated by turning ideas into practical and reliable software.
